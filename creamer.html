Pump & Single Squeeze

Having the freedom of keeping your creamer on a counter top or hidden inside a drawer is something unheard of. Until NOW! Splash comes in two conveninent presentation.
Single Squeeze bottles, sold in 12 packs and single units.  This are perfect to carry in your purse or your backpack. And 22 Servings will last you quite a while!

And if that wasn't convenient enough, we also have Single Serve Squeeze Pods which are premeasured liquid coffee pods perfect to prepare a cup of coffee on the go. These are perfect to carry in your bag, backpack or your car.  


Our coffee concentrates are processed and manufactured using a low-temperature brew method that preserves the natural flavor and aroma of the coffee beans. The coffee concentrate simply needs to be mixed with hot or cold water, eliminating the many drawbacks of Brewing Systems:

Investment Savings: no need to go out and spend hundreds of dollars on a fancy brewing system. With any hot water source, or a microwave for heating water, you can enjoy a delicious specialty coffee beverage with just our Top Roast mix.
Refill Savings: most refill pods are priced expensively and designed to only work with specific equipment. Because we designed our coffee to be super-concentrated, each serving is only 5ml (roughly 1/6 of an ounce). This efficiency allows us to pack 100 servings in each bottle at a tremendous value.
Space Savings: most brewing systems are bulky, difficult to move, and constantly have to be connected to a power source. Instead, you can put Top Roast anywhere in the house or at your workplace. Even try keeping it on your desk next to your water for a quick, refreshing pick-me-up!
